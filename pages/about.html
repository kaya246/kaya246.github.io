Kaya Ramirez
about me
I’m a 25 year old designer from New York. I began studying architecture at Brooklyn Technical High School, later getting my bachelors degree from the University of Michigan in 2020.

I have worked at several design firms including KM, A and SO-IL, for artists Paul Pfieffer and Sagarika Sundaram, and for myself. My work has been featured at the Brooklyn Botanic Garden, the Los Angeles Museum of Contemporary Art, and the Storefront for Art And Architecture.

My favorite thing to do in my free time is play piano. I also like to ski, make pizza, take photos, and do crosswords.
