Kitchen / Stair
Prospect Heights, Brooklyn
This kitchen and stair overhaul explores the interaction between materials and movement through space. Paperstone seamlessly clads the entire space, navigating guests and residents around the circular island made of fluted walnut. Design work was done under architect Idan Naor.

Additionally, I spent two months building and installing the millwork and staircase with carpenter Jesse Lane.
