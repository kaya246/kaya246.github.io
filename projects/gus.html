Upper West Side Apartment
Spring 2022
As space in Manhattan is scarce, New Yorkers must turn to creative solutions. By taking advantage of the high ceilings, and exposing some of the joists, we were able to build a loft bed that has standing room around it.

In doing so, the 350 square foot apartment manages to have a spacious kitchen and living room with full size appliances and a washer/dryer. As well as an office, and a large bathroom.
Kaya Ramirez
